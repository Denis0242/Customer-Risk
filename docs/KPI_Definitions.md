# KPI Definitions — Customer Risk Analysis

| KPI | Definition | Business Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Total Customers | Count of unique customer IDs | Measures customer base represented in claims data |
| Average Loss per Customer | Total claim amount / unique customers | Shows financial exposure per customer |
| Total Loss | Sum of claim amount | Measures total claim cost exposure |
| High-Risk Customer % | High-risk customers / total customers | Tracks concentration of risky customers |
| Claims Frequency | Count of claim records | Identifies claim-heavy groups |
| Loss Contribution | Sum of claim amount by segment | Shows which segments drive cost |
| Risk Score | Weighted score using claim frequency and loss | Prioritizes customer review |
