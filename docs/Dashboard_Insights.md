# Dashboard Insights — Customer Risk Analysis

## Key Insight
High-risk customers represent 42.64% of customers under the project thresholds and drive concentrated loss exposure.

## Action
Focus monitoring on high-risk customers and track medium-risk customers closely before they become high-risk.

## Recommendation
Adjust pricing, strengthen underwriting, and target high-risk regions and policy types.

## Decision
Prioritize high-risk customer review and intervention, implement stricter underwriting for high-risk segments, and launch risk mitigation programs for medium-risk customers.
